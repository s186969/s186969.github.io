About

We imagine a world where there’s no such thing as a bad food and that farmers live prosperously.

Our values include providing fair wages for workers, giving back to the communities we work with, and serving great coffee.

At Adare, we wish to offer our personal rendition of Nordic gourmet cuisine with an innovative gastronomic take on traditional cooking methods, fine Nordic produce and the legacy of our shared food heritage. Moreover, we regard it as a personal challenge to help bring about a revival of Nordic cuisine and let its distinctive flavours and particular regional character brighten up the world.

We would be thrilled to invite you to our restaurant in the central area of Oslo. We are looking forward to your stay.
